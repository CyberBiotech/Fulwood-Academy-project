Details about Fulwood Academy

Fulwood Academy is a school with essential values such as:We Care, We Challenge, We Commit. Fulwood Academy is rated by OFSTED as a 'good' school. The learing is exquisite, the hygiene is ungodly, and the kindness is spread across the whole school from staff to students and from students to staff. They have extracurricular activities like afterschool clubs to help with your learning towards each assessment point. These assessment points are known as 'AP'. There are three 'APs' in a school year and they are: AP1, AP2 and AP3. Each assessment point happens in a term. 'AP1' happens in the 1st term, 'AP2' happens in the 2nd term and 'Ap3' happens in the 3rd term.

Details about the Fulwood Academy education and curriculum

Fulwood Academy's curriculum, built on the principles of "We Care, We Challenge, We Commit," aims to develop well-rounded individuals. It features sequenced, powerful knowledge specified in detail to enhance long-term retention, supported by home learning strategies. The Teach SMART approach ensures high expectations, quality instruction, regular testing, adaptive learning, and clear feedback. Core subjects include English, Mathematics, and Science, with options in humanities, languages, arts, and vocational studies.

Fulwood Academy achievements

Fulwood Academy has made significant strides in recent years, achieving its best Ofsted rating in 15 years with a "Good" rating across the board in 2023. This improvement follows a period of focused efforts to enhance educational standards and create a harmonious learning environment. The academy has been recognized for its outstanding efforts, positive relationships between staff and students, and strong support for pupils with special educational needs. Under the leadership of Principal Andrew Galbraith, Fulwood Academy has demonstrated a relentless pursuit of excellence, resulting in a respectful and accomplished school community.
